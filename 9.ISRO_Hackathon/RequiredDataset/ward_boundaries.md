Dataset: Ward / Municipal Boundaries

Official sources / notes:

- Ward boundaries are typically available from municipal corporations (city government portals) or local GIS departments.
- Alternative sources: OpenStreetMap (extract via Overpass/API) or municipal open data portals.

Notes / access:

- These are local files that must be downloaded from the city (Thane municipal portal) or extracted from OSM and then stored under `data/raw/wards/`.
