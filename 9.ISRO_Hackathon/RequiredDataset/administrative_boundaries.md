Dataset: Administrative Boundaries

Official sources:

- geoBoundaries: https://www.geoboundaries.org/
- GADM: https://gadm.org/

Notes / access:

- Prefer geoBoundaries or GADM for country/state/district polygons. Download GeoJSON/Shapefile for India and load as vector layers.
- For Earth Engine, you can upload boundaries as assets or use any available GEE dataset if appropriate.
